Objectives
The event we are going to predict is : "Which team will be at the top of the DLF Indian Premier League 2012 points table before April 30th".

You will be given $1000 electronic cash. Your aim is to buy as many shares of the winning team as possible.

A share of a team that wins will fetch you $1. The player who has the highest number of shares of the winning team wins!
Lots of attractive prices are in store!
How to Play
Once you enter the trading area, you will find a list of the IPL Teams. Current Share Price represents the value of a share of the particular team. If you feel that this value should increase- thereby implying that the team has a greater chance of winning- you should simply give a higher value in the price goes to... section. Similarly, if you feel that the price of the share should go down, give a value lower than the Current Share Price in price goes to...


Note that the Share Price must lie between 0 and 100 cents, excluding 0 and 100 (in way, the share price reflects the probability of that particular team winning).
In addition to the Trading Table, you'll be given a list of the shares you presently hold, as well as your Trading History.

The Total Cost tells you the amount of money you've spent so far. Mind you, if you click on Sell Holdings, then you're selling ALL shares of that particular team. So exercise this button with caution!
Your objective would be to drive the market price of the shares of the team you think is going to win.
You shouldn't really be worried about the number of shares you are owning. The software takes care of all that on its own.
